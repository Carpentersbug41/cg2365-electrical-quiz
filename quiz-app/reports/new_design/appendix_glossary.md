# Glossary Appendix

## Chunk
A bounded instructional unit containing one short teaching move and the metadata needed to ask, repair, and advance.

## Teaching Core
The short pre-generated instructional text used to introduce the chunk content.

## Repair
A short corrective interaction after weak or wrong performance, usually consisting of correction, contrast, and retry.

## Microbreak Exercise
A brief instructional exercise used to interrupt long text-heavy or repair-heavy sequences and reduce fatigue.

## Learner-Performance Measurement
Data used to adapt instruction for an individual learner.

## App-Performance Measurement
Data used to evaluate and improve the product itself.

## Support Dependence
The extent to which success depends on hints, repair, or other scaffolds.

## Transfer
The learner's ability to use knowledge in a new context rather than merely recall it.

## Recovery Capacity
The learner's ability to recover from failure and continue productively.

## Fatigue Onset
The point in a session where behavioural indicators suggest productive effort is turning into fatigue.

## Runtime Version
The version label for the runtime behavior being measured.

## Variant ID
The identifier for a specific prompt/UI/runtime experiment version.

## Change ID
The identifier for a shipped product change so its results can be measured and traced.
